Storyboards

First Storyboard

During this feedback session, my partner suggested that in addition to allowing users to generate recipes by culture, I could also add a continent filter, where they can select a recipe from a specific continent. He also raised a concern of when there are duplicate recipe posts, what would happen? These ideas helped me think of how certain continents might not have access to certain ingredients and how I would deal with duplicate posts or recipes by different users.

Second Storyboard

In this feedback session, my partner didn't have much feedback to give, which could mean that my improved storyboard was pretty thorough. The only concern she had was whether I could broaden the types of food to posts, instead of just desserts. This is something that I did think of, but my idea for that was slightly more complicated. Focusing on just cultural desserts made more sense for me because my purpose was for the site to be a place where people can compare different cultural food and notice the obvious differences.
