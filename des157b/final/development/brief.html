Project Brief

Summary

My project will be a communal blog site, where users from all around the world can share their cultural desserts and recipes, so that people can explore and understand the cultural differences in food.

Goals

The goal of my project is to allow people to evaluate the cultural differences in desserts, explore the different methods of baking, and understand and appreciate the uniqueness of each culture's food.

For my blog, instead of one person posting, I want to allow multiple users to post, so that people can also learn how people with a specific cultural background make their food. Nuts About Greens is a blog site that posts many healthy and vegan friendly recipes. Users could generate certain recipes by category, which is similar to my idea, but instead, users will be filtering by culture and continent.

Design Strategies

I am aiming to design a blog site that is minimalistic, neat, and welcoming. Because it is a site where any user can post to it, I want to keep the interface as simple as possible, so it is not overwhelming or hard to navigate. I will be using a grey and white themed color palette to set a calming, clean mood for the page and I will be using friendly typefaces to incorporate the welcoming idea.

Interaction Strategies

To simplify to process and avoid users from having to create an account or login, I think I will prompt the user to fill out a form if they want to share a recipe. I am planning on having a navigation bar at the top of the page and checkbox filter options on the left side. The center of the page will be a grid layout of posted dessert recipes that people can click on.

Experience Goals

Through this communal blog, I hope that users who share their cultural desserts will become confident and proud about their culture. They will be able to accomplish this by filling out a form to post to the page. I also hope that users who stumble upon this page can not only appreciate cultural foods, but also learn and respect the cultural differences from around the world. To make the comparisons easier, this blog will only allow users to share desserts.
