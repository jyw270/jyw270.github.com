Comparative Analysis

Curious Cuisinière

This blog site posts cultural recipes from all around the world. The purpose of this site is to allow people to explore different cuisines and understand the cultural history behind each dish. This is a perfect example and reference for me, but instead of one person posting, I want to allow multiple users to post, so that people can also learn how people with a specific cultural background make their food. Other than that, I found this website, so interesting because their are such a wide range of food and they are all organized into visually appealing categories.

Nuts About Greens

This blog site posts many healthy and vegan friendly recipes. The interface is very clean and neat, making it easy for users to navigate around. I like how users could generate certain recipes by category, which is similar to my idea, but instead, users will be filtering by culture and continent. Overall, I really like the simplicity and aesthetic of this site. I find it very inspiring.
