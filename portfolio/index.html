About

Hello, I'm Jenny! I graduated from UC Davis with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Computer Science. I am passionate about front-end web development and UX/UI design because they are a great combination of my interests.

My goal as a designer and developer is to create applications and websites that bring comfort and happiness to people. I genuinely value the importance of empathizing with users in order to create effective products that are familiar and satisfying. My design aesthetics revolves around simplicity and elegance, which my logo portrays as a simple flower with vibrant, delicate colors. The stem represents the letter, J in my first name, while the subtle heart shape in the flower represents the word 爱 (love) in my Chinese name.
