About Me

Hello, I'm Jenny! I am currently an undergraduate student at UC Davis, majoring in Design and minoring in Computer Science. I am passionate about Front-end web development and UX/UI design because they are a great combination of my interests.

My goal as a designer is to create applications and websites that can make an impact on the world and create comfort for people. My design aesthetic revolves around simplicity and elegance, which my logo portrays as a simple flower with vibrant, delicate colors. The stem represents the letter, J in my first name, while the subtle heart shape in the flower represents the character Áà± (love) in my Chinese name.
