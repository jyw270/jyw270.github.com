ConnectMe

My role: Designer

Year: 2022

Tools: Figma

Overview

A social app for UC Davis students to be able to find friends and connect with other people of similar interests. Group project prototyping done over the course of 10 weeks.

Define

We understood that the main frustration was that students struggled to create and maintain friendships virtually because they were not familiar with this form of communication.

Design System

The majority of my team members weren’t familiar with Figma and other design tools, so I addressed this issue by creating a design system to ensure consistent designs and allow us to complete this project in an efficient and timely manner.

Prototype

01

Creating an account/setting up profile

We followed common design patterns to make the log in process feel familiar to users. To create an account, one must have a valid UC Davis email, in order to limit the app to UC Davis students only.

02

Add additional information to their profile

Once a user creates their profile, they can further customize it by adding their pronouns, bio, hobbies, pictures, and tags about their interests, which is used to filter potential friends with similar information.

03

Find a friend through filter options

When searching for potential friends, we decided to tailor the filter options based on the user’s interests. Then, based on the filters selected, we implemented the match percentage to result in more meaningful matches.

04

Interactions with a new friend

In the chat feature, once a user accepts a friend request, they are given a certain amount of time to decide whether they bond with that person or not. If not, the app will automatically remove that person when the timer expires, designed to avoid hurt feelings. If a user deicides to keep a friend, additional features such as a recommended activities list becomes available to encourage in-person interaction.

Challenges and Further Development

Challenges

  • Simulating an in-person friendship online through limited interaction was difficult
  • Majority of team members weren’t familiar with Figma and other design tools
  • Time constraint

Further Development

  • Conduct usability tests and revisions
  • Expanding our audience to students in other universities
  • Refine our prototype and including more details, such as sharing life experiences and class schedules
  • Promote school events for students to join and explore their interests
