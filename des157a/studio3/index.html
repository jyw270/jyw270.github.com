If You Give A Bunny A Carrot

Rules

  • there are two players
  • you have the option to roll the random carrot generator or pass your turn
  • each time you roll, the total number of carrots in the baskets will be added to your score
  • if you roll a 1, your turn is passed to the other player
  • if you roll two 1s, your score gets zeroed out
  • when it is your turn, you can continue rolling again and again as long as you don't roll a 1
  • the first player to feed all 30 of their bunnies wins
